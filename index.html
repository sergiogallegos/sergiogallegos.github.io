About Me

I am a Controls & Automation Engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Electromechanical Engineering, currently pursuing a Master of Science in Software Engineering (MSSwE). With over a decade of experience in industrial automation, I specialize in PLC programming, motion control, vision systems, and robotic integration.

Alongside my professional work, I am deeply committed to expanding my software development skills. My journey includes both formal education and self-directed learning, driven by a strong passion for innovation, systems programming, and the fusion of software with industrial technology.

My interests span across:

  • Automation Machinery Design & Control
  • Software Engineering & System Design
  • Machine Learning & Robotics
  • Embedded Systems & System Programming
  • Web Development & Data-Driven Applications

I strive to bridge the gap between industrial control systems and modern software engineering, building scalable, intelligent solutions that drive real-world impact.
